VERSION 1.0.0

A first of its kind speech to sign language translator for India. If one of your friends or loved ones is deaf or has a hearing impairment, it is quite difficult to communicate with them if you don't know the sign language. Worry no more, Aidable is here for your aid.

Aidable is a web app that interprets spoken and written English into Indian sign language using a 3D friendly avatar. The 3D avatar will interpret text/speech input in English and deliver real-time sign language translations, enabling easier communication with the deaf & hard of hearing community without having to know sign language. Aidable is not meant to replace human SL interpreters, rather to make every-day communication with the deaf and hard of hearing community more accessible.

Two input ways

Aidable also translates text into sign lanuage, so the user can type as well as speak as per their convinence.

Learn Sign Language

Aidable features a Indian Sign Language tutorial with multiple reference links, making it a great tool for anyone to learn the language from their phones.

Aidable is a powerful tool, used in the most diverse contexts:

  • In the classroom, by teachers, students and interpreters as a complementary communication resource.
  • At home, by families with deaf and hearing people.
  • For learning Indian Sign Language.

Main Features

  • Real-Time English-to-Indian Sign Language Translation (speech/text-to-SL)
  • Indian Sign Language Tutorial
  • Multiple 3D avatars
